About Ancient Korean Life Reading

Ancient Korean Life Reading is a modern English interpretation inspired by Tojeong Bigyeol, a traditional Korean symbolic reading system historically associated with the Joseon-era scholar Yi Ji-ham, also known as Tojeong.

For generations, many Koreans explored this tradition near the beginning of the year as a way to reflect on timing, priorities, relationships, and personal direction.

This website presents the tradition as cultural and symbolic guidance for reflection, curiosity, and personal insight. It is not scientific prediction, financial advice, or a guarantee of future outcomes.
