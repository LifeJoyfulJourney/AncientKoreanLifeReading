    Cultural origin

    What is Tojeong Bigyeol?

    Tojeong Bigyeol is a traditional Korean annual reading system associated with the Joseon-era scholar Yi Ji-ham, known as Tojeong.

    Historically, people consulted it near the beginning of the year to reflect on fortune, timing, relationships, and life direction.

    This website presents it as symbolic cultural guidance rather than scientific prediction.

    Respectful cultural guidance

    Why people return each year

    Annual reflection

    Many Koreans revisit Tojeong Bigyeol yearly as a reflective tradition to think about timing, priorities, and direction.

    Cultural note

    This site presents a historic Korean symbolic tradition in modern English form with a calm, respectful interpretation style.

    How accurate is it?

    This is a symbolic interpretive system, not scientific certainty. Use it for reflection, cultural interest, and thoughtful conversation.
